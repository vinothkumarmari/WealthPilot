school {{ t('How MCX & COMEX Work — Gold Price Explained') }}
language COMEX (New York)

What: The world's largest gold futures exchange, part of CME Group (Chicago Mercantile Exchange). Located in New York, USA.

How it works: Traders buy/sell contracts for gold delivery at a future date. One contract = 100 troy ounces (~3.1 kg). Prices are in USD per troy ounce.

Trading hours: Sun–Fri, 6:00 PM – 5:00 PM ET (next day). Almost 23 hours/day.

Why it matters for India:

  • COMEX sets the global benchmark price for gold
  • Indian gold prices follow COMEX + USD/INR exchange rate + import duty + GST
  • When COMEX rises at night (US time), Indian gold opens higher next morning
  • Formula: Indian gold ≈ (COMEX price ÷ 31.1g) × USD/INR × (1 + import duty + GST)

Example: If COMEX gold = $4,750/oz and USD/INR = 93.5, then: $4,750 ÷ 31.1 × 93.5 = ₹14,280/gram (before duties). IBJA adds ~₹900 premium = ₹15,180/gram.

account_balance MCX (Mumbai)

What: Multi Commodity Exchange of India. India's largest commodity exchange, regulated by SEBI. Located in Mumbai.

How it works: Traders buy/sell gold futures contracts. MCX Gold = 1 kg, MCX Gold Mini = 100g, MCX Gold Petal = 1g. Prices are in ₹ per 10 grams.

Trading hours: Mon–Fri, 9:00 AM – 11:30 PM IST (extended session till 11:55 PM).

Why it matters for you:

  • MCX price = COMEX price converted to INR + India premium
  • Jewellers and bullion dealers use MCX to hedge their gold inventory
  • Your gold chit company likely bases rates on IBJA, which tracks MCX
  • MCX night session (after 5 PM) mirrors COMEX live — prices can change overnight

GOLDBEES ETF: Nippon India Gold ETF on NSE. 1 unit ≈ price of ~0.01g gold. We use this as a real-time MCX proxy since MCX direct data requires paid access.

menu_book Key Terms
Troy Ounce
31.1035 grams. COMEX uses this, not regular ounces (28.35g).
Spot Price
Current price for immediate delivery. Changes every second during trading.
Futures Price
Price agreed today for delivery in a future month. Usually slightly higher than spot.
India Premium
Extra cost of gold in India vs international price. Includes import duty, GST, demand-supply. Typically ₹200-1,500/gram.
IBJA Rate
India Bullion and Jewellers Association sets daily gold rates at 10:30 AM and 3:00 PM. Most jewellers follow this.
GOLDBEES / Gold ETF
Exchange-traded fund backed by physical gold. Tracks gold price without making charges. 1 unit ≈ 0.01g gold.
999 / 995 / 916 Purity
999 = 24K (99.9% pure). 916 = 22K (jewellery grade). 750 = 18K.
Making Charges
Jeweller's fee for crafting. 8-25% for ornaments. 0% for gold coins/bars.
tips_and_updates Smart Buying Tips
Best time of day:
  • AM rates (10:30 AM) are usually cheaper than PM (3:00 PM)
  • Sunday night COMEX opening can cause Monday AM gaps
  • Avoid buying when US markets just opened (8:30 PM IST)
Best day of week:
  • Monday/Tuesday often see dips after weekend consolidation
  • Friday prices can be volatile (US jobs data)
  • Avoid Akshaya Tritiya / Dhanteras (premium spikes)
Gold chit strategy:
  • Pay early in the month if prices are rising
  • Use our AM/PM pattern to pick the cheaper session
  • If India premium is high (>₹1,000), consider Gold ETF instead
