rocket_launchGetting Started
  1. Set up your Profile: Click Profile in the sidebar. Enter your monthly salary, age, and risk appetite. This data powers AI suggestions, budget analysis, and retirement planning.
  2. Add your Income: Go to Income & Salary and record your salary and other income sources.
  3. Track Expenses: Navigate to Expenses and start logging daily expenses by category.
  4. Record Investments: Add your existing investments, insurance policies, schemes, and SIPs.
  5. Check AI Suggestions: Visit AI Suggestions to get personalized investment and savings recommendations.
lightbulb Sidebar Navigation: The sidebar is organized into 3 sections — Finance (Dashboard, Income, Expenses, Budget, Loans), Investments (Investments, Policies, Schemes, SIP, Gold & Silver, Assets, Goals), and Tools & Info (Tax Planning, Calculators, AI Suggestions, Reports, Rate Monitor, Govt Schemes, Indian Budget).
dashboardDashboard

Your financial overview at a glance.

  • Summary Cards: Total Income, Expenses, Investments, and Net Worth displayed at the top.
  • Net Worth: Calculated as Investments + Bank Balances + Provident Fund – Loan Outstanding amounts. Gives a true picture of your financial position.
  • Monthly Trends: Chart showing income vs expenses over the last 6 months.
  • Quick Actions: Fast links to add income, expense, or investment.
  • Due Reminders: Upcoming premium payments and SIP dates.
  • Maturity Tracker: Policies and schemes nearing maturity.
account_balance_walletIncome & Salary

Record and manage all your income sources.

  • Add Income: Click "Add Income" button. Select month, enter amount, source (Salary, Freelance, Rental, etc.), and optional description.
  • Monthly View: See income breakdown by month with totals.
  • Income Sources: Track multiple income streams — primary salary, side income, rental income, dividends.
  • Edit/Delete: Click the action buttons next to each entry to modify or remove.
lightbulb Tip: Keep your salary updated in Profile for accurate budget analysis and AI suggestions.
receiptExpenses

Track spending with automatic budget analysis.

  • Add Expense: Enter amount, category (Housing, Food, Transport, Entertainment, etc.), date, and description.
  • Budget Analysis (50/30/20 Rule): Your expenses are auto-categorized into Needs, Wants, and Savings. The system compares your actual spending against the 50/30/20 benchmark.
  • Warning Alerts: Red alerts appear when Needs exceeds 50%, Wants exceeds 30%, or Savings drops below 20% of income.
  • Monthly Budget Trend: Stacked bar chart shows how your budget split changed over the last 6 months.
  • Monthly Commitments: Sidebar shows your insurance, scheme, SIP, and EMI commitments deducted from salary.
  • Category Breakdown: Doughnut chart shows spending by category.
warning Note: Categories are auto-mapped: Housing/Food/Transport/Utilities/Healthcare = Needs, Entertainment/Shopping/Personal Care = Wants, Savings/Investments/Debt Payments = Savings.
trending_upInvestments

Manage your investment portfolio including policies, schemes, and SIPs.

  • Add Investment: Enter name, type (Stocks, Mutual Funds, FD, Gold, etc.), amount invested, current value, and date.
  • Unified View: The investments page shows regular investments along with your insurance policies (as Insurance badge), government schemes (as Scheme badge), and SIPs (as SIP badge).
  • Portfolio Allocation: Pie chart shows how your money is distributed across investment types.
  • Profit/Loss: Each investment shows gain/loss amount and percentage in real-time.
  • Family Tracking: Assign investments to different family members.
securityInsurance Policies

Track all your insurance policies and premium payments.

  • Add Policy: Enter policy name, company, type (Life/Health/Vehicle/Term), policy number, premium amount, frequency (Monthly/Quarterly/Yearly), start & maturity dates, and sum assured.
  • Premium Tracking: Record premium payments with date and amount. Premium payments are automatically linked as expenses.
  • Maturity Alerts: Policies nearing maturity are highlighted on the dashboard.
  • Due Date Reminders: Get notified when premiums are due.
  • Document Upload: Upload policy documents for easy reference.
workspace_premiumSchemes & Bonds

Track personal savings schemes and browse a directory of government & private bonds.

  • Add Scheme: Enter scheme name, type (PPF, NSC, KVP, SSY, SCSS, Bonds, etc.), invested amount, current value, interest rate, start & maturity dates.
  • Interest Tracking: Monitor accumulated interest and maturity value.
  • Premium Payments: Record periodic deposits for schemes like PPF.
  • Maturity Tracker: Get alerts when schemes are maturing.
  • Bonds Directory: Browse government bonds (RBI Floating Rate, G-Sec, SDLs, T-Bills, NABARD, 54EC Capital Gain Bonds, IRFC Tax-Free) and top private/corporate NCDs (HDFC, Bajaj Finance, Tata Capital, Shriram Finance, Muthoot Finance, Mahindra Finance) with ratings, yields, and direct buy links.
  • Bond Tips: Guidance on buying AAA/AA+ rated bonds via platforms like TheFixedIncome.com, IndiaBonds.com, or RBI Retail Direct.
loopSIP Tracker

Track your Systematic Investment Plans alongside policy and scheme recurring investments.

  • Add SIP: Enter fund name, platform (Groww, Zerodha, etc.), SIP amount, SIP date, expected return %, total invested, and current value.
  • Unified View: Shows regular SIPs, plus active insurance policies (with premium frequency) and recurring scheme investments.
  • Performance: Track actual returns vs expected returns for each SIP.
  • Active/Inactive: Toggle SIP status. Inactive SIPs are tracked but excluded from commitment calculations.
calculateBudget Planner

Plan monthly budgets by category and track spending against planned amounts.

  • Set Budget: Define planned amounts for each spending category (Housing, Food, Transport, etc.) per month.
  • Track vs Actual: Compare planned budget against actual spending for each category.
  • Monthly Planning: Set budgets month by month to accommodate seasonal changes.
credit_scoreLoans

Track all your loans with EMI schedules, interest analysis, and AI-powered good/bad assessment.

  • Add Loans: Track Home, Car, Personal, Education, Gold, Credit Card, and Business loans.
  • EMI Tracking: Record monthly EMI payments and see remaining balance with progress bar.
  • AI Analysis: Each loan gets a score (Good/Average/Bad) based on interest rate, EMI-to-income ratio, and loan type.
  • Credit Card Offers: Get AI-recommended credit cards based on your income and spending profile.
  • Prepayment Tips: AI suggests how much you can save by paying extra each month.
diamondGold & Silver Rates

Track gold and silver prices with multi-karat charts, trend analysis, and AI insights.

  • Multi-Karat Rates: Current prices for Gold 24K, 22K, and 18K (per gram & per 10g) with daily change percentage and trend badges.
  • Silver Rates: Silver per gram and per kg with daily change and trend analysis.
  • Interactive Charts: Toggle between 30 Days, 12 Months, and 5 Years views. Gold chart shows all 3 karats overlaid for comparison.
  • AI Insights: Separate gold and silver investment tips — buy/hold/wait recommendations based on trends.
  • Investment Links: Direct links to invest in Sovereign Gold Bond, Gold ETF, Gold Mutual Fund, Silver ETF, and Silver Coins.
  • Market Data: Prices sourced from live market data (bullions.co.in) and synced via the Rate Monitor.
monitoringRate Monitor

Central dashboard showing all government scheme rates, tax slabs, commodity prices, and FD rates — auto-synced across the app.

  • Small Savings: PPF, SSY, SCSS, NSC, KVP, MIS, Time Deposits — all rates from NSI India.
  • PF Rates: Current EPF, VPF, GPF interest rates.
  • Tax Slabs: New regime income tax slabs for FY 2025-26.
  • Commodity Rates: Gold (24K, 22K, 18K per gram) and Silver (per gram/kg) base prices — these feed into the Gold & Silver page charts and analysis.
  • FD Rates: Top bank FD rates (SBI, HDFC, ICICI, Axis, Kotak, PNB).
  • Govt Links: Quick-access links to all official government portals (Income Tax, EPFO, NPS, NSI, etc.).
  • Auto-Sync: All rates here flow into AI suggestions, investment analysis, and scheme pages.
account_balanceBank Accounts

Track your savings/current/salary account balances across banks.

  • Add Account: Store bank name, account type, last 4 digits, and current balance.
  • Update Balance: Quickly update balance inline from the investments page.
  • AI Integration: Bank balance is included in net worth calculations and AI suggestions.
savingsProvident Fund

Track EPF, VPF, and GPF accounts with contribution details.

  • PF Types: EPF (Employee PF), VPF (Voluntary PF), GPF (Government PF).
  • Contributions: Track your monthly employee + employer contributions.
  • Balance: Total PF balance is shown in investments and included in net worth.
  • Interest Rate: Current EPF rate (8.25%) is auto-synced from Rate Monitor.
receiptTax Planning

Plan your tax savings and compare old vs new tax regimes.

  • Tax Deductions: View available deductions under Section 80C, 80D, 80CCD, 24(b), etc.
  • Auto-Mapping: Your existing investments (PPF, ELSS, NPS, insurance premiums) are auto-mapped to tax sections.
  • Regime Comparison: Compare tax liability under Old Regime vs New Regime to pick the best option.
  • Gap Analysis: See how much more you can invest to maximize tax savings.
account_balanceGovernment Schemes & Subsidies

Comprehensive directory of government schemes and subsidies across India.

  • Central Schemes: PPF, NSC, SSY, SCSS, POMIS, KVP, NPS, APY, SGB, POTD, Post Office RD, Mahila Samman, PMVVY — with interest rates, tenure, and eligibility details.
  • State Schemes: Filter by state to view state-specific schemes (Tamil Nadu, Karnataka, Kerala, Maharashtra, AP, Telangana, Gujarat).
  • Central Subsidies: PM Awas Yojana (housing), PM Ujjwala (LPG), PM Kisan (farmer income), Mudra Yojana (business loans), Ayushman Bharat (health), PM Fasal Bima (crop insurance), PMJJBY/PMSBY (life & accident cover), PM Surya Ghar (solar), EV subsidies.
  • State Subsidies: State-level subsidies — TN agriculture electricity, KA Gruha Jyothi/Lakshmi, MH Ladki Bahin, TS Rythu Bandhu, AP YSR Cheyutha, KL electricity subsidy.
  • Apply Online: Each scheme has an "Apply Online" button that redirects to the official government portal.
  • Search & Filter: Use the dropdown filter (All / Central / State / Subsidies) and search bar to find schemes quickly.
Note: Bonds (Government & Private) have been moved to the Schemes & Bonds page for better organization.
receipt_longIndian Budget

Key highlights from the Union Budget 2025-26 that impact your investments.

  • Tax Slabs: New regime income tax slabs with zero tax up to ₹12,75,000 for salaried individuals.
  • TDS/TCS Changes: Updated threshold limits for TDS on rent, interest, dividends, etc.
  • Capital Gains: Updated STCG/LTCG rates and indexation rules.
  • NPS Vatsalya: New NPS accounts for minors.
  • Action Links: Direct links to file ITR, use tax calculator, and access budget documents.
calculateCalculators

Financial calculators for planning and analysis.

  • SIP Calculator: Calculate future value of SIP investments with expected returns.
  • EMI Calculator: Calculate monthly EMI for loans based on amount, tenure, and interest rate.
  • FD Calculator: Calculate maturity amount for Fixed Deposits.
  • Retirement Calculator: Estimate required retirement corpus based on current expenses and inflation.
psychologyAI Suggestions

Personalized AI-powered financial recommendations.

  • Investment Plan: Based on your age, salary, and risk appetite, the AI suggests specific investments (SIPs, PPF, NPS, etc.) with exact monthly amounts.
  • Commodity Suggestions: Gold, Silver, and commodity investment options with trend analysis and apply links.
  • Asset Buying Plans: Recommends asset purchases (car, house, bike, land, farm) you can afford based on EMI capacity. Shows down payment, loan amount, tenure, and tips.
  • Vehicle Buy Timing: AI suggests the best time to buy a car or bike based on seasonal patterns (festive discounts, year-end clearance, budget season). Shows tips for car/bike purchases.
  • Retirement Planning: Shows required corpus, monthly SIP needed, and years to retirement.
  • Tax Saving Tips: Suggests tax-saving instruments under 80C, 80CCD, and 80D with lock-in periods and returns.
  • Investment Comparison Table: Compare all investment options by risk, return, tenure, and liquidity.
  • Apply Buttons: Each suggestion has an "Apply" button to directly open the relevant investment platform.
warning Note: If your salary is not set, Asset Buying Plans will show a prompt to update your Profile.
lightbulb Tip: Update your age, salary, and risk appetite in Profile for more accurate recommendations.
real_estate_agentAssets

Track your physical and financial assets.

  • Add Asset: Enter asset type (Real Estate, Vehicle, Electronics, Jewelry, etc.), name, purchase price, current value, and purchase date.
  • EMI Tracking: For financed assets, enter loan amount, EMI amount, and remaining months.
  • Appreciation/Depreciation: Track value changes over time.
  • Asset Distribution: View portfolio allocation across asset types.
warning Note: Leave optional fields (current value, EMI, loan amount) empty if not applicable — they default to 0.

Set and track financial goals.

  • Create Goal: Set a goal name, target amount, target date, and current saved amount.

personProfile Settings

Manage your account settings and personal information.

lock_resetForgot Password

Reset your password securely via email OTP verification.

  • Step 1: Click "Forgot Password?" on the Login page.
  • Step 2: Enter your registered email address.
  • Step 3: A {{ config.OTP_LENGTH }}-digit OTP will be sent to your email (or displayed if SMTP is not configured).
  • Step 4: Enter the OTP to verify your identity.
  • Step 5: Set a new password (minimum 6 characters) and confirm it.
  • Resend OTP: If you don't receive the OTP, click "Resend OTP" to get a new one.
