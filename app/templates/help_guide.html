rocket_launchGetting Started
  1. Create Account: Register with your email. A 6-digit OTP will be sent for verification — we ensure every account is verified.
  2. Set up your Profile: Click Profile in the sidebar. Enter your monthly salary, age, and risk appetite. This data powers AI suggestions, budget analysis, and retirement planning.
  3. Add your Income: Go to Income & Salary and record your salary and other income sources.
  4. Track Expenses: Navigate to Expenses and start logging daily expenses by category.
  5. Record Investments: Add your existing investments, insurance policies, schemes, and SIPs.
  6. Check AI Suggestions: Visit AI Suggestions to get personalized investment and savings recommendations.
  7. Enable Notifications: The notification bell in the top bar will alert you about due premiums, SIP dates, goal deadlines, and loan EMIs.
lightbulb Sidebar Navigation: The sidebar is organized into 3 sections — Finance (Dashboard, Income, Expenses, Budget, Loans), Investments (Investments, Policies, Schemes, SIP, Gold & Silver, Assets, Goals), and Tools & Info (Tax Planning, Calculators, AI Suggestions, Business Ideas, Reports, Rate Monitor, Govt Schemes, Indian Budget, Notifications).

  • Warning Alerts: Red alerts appear when Needs exceeds 50%, Wants exceeds 30%, or Savings drops below 20% of income.

  • Search & Filter: Filter expenses by category, amount, or description.
warning Note: Categories are auto-mapped: Housing/Food/Transport/Utilities/Healthcare = Needs, Entertainment/Shopping/Personal Care = Wants, Savings/Investments/Debt Payments = Savings.

securitySecurity & Privacy

WealthPilot is built with security as a core principle. Here's how we protect your data:

shield Security Measures
  • CSRF Protection: Every form and API call is protected against Cross-Site Request Forgery attacks.
  • Password Hashing: Passwords are hashed using industry-standard algorithms — we never store plaintext passwords.
  • OTP Verification: 6-digit OTP with 5-minute expiry for email verification and password resets.
  • Rate Limiting: Login and registration are rate-limited to prevent brute-force attacks.
  • Session Security: Sessions are managed securely with automatic expiry.
  • Input Validation: All user inputs are validated server-side to prevent injection attacks.
  • File Upload Security: Only approved file types (PDF, images) up to 16MB. All filenames are sanitized.
privacy_tip Privacy Commitment
  • No Third-Party Sharing: Your financial data is never sold, shared, or transmitted to any third-party service.
  • No Tracking: We don't use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any third-party tracking scripts.
  • No Ads: WealthPilot is completely ad-free.
  • Local Processing: All AI recommendations are processed by our own engine — no data sent to external AI services.
  • Minimal Data Collection: We only collect data you explicitly enter. No background data harvesting.
  • Admin Controlled: Only authorized administrators can access the admin panel.
install_mobileInstall as App (PWA)

  2. Click the install icon (⊕) in the address bar

  4. WealthPilot opens as a standalone app window
