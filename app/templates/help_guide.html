smsSMS Import & Auto-Sync New

Automatically track expenses and income by reading bank transaction SMS — no manual entry needed.

content_paste Manual SMS Paste (Web)
  • How to use: On the Expenses or Income page, click the SMS button in the header.
  • Paste SMS: Copy-paste your bank SMS messages into the text area. You can paste multiple messages at once.
  • AI Parsing: If a Gemini API key is configured, SMS is parsed using Google Gemini AI for high accuracy. Otherwise, regex-based parsing is used.
  • Review & Edit: Parsed transactions are shown in a review panel. You can edit the category, date, amount, and description before adding.
  • Supported Banks: SBI, HDFC, ICICI, Axis, Kotak, PNB, BOB, Canara, and 20+ Indian banks and UPI apps.
  • Auto-Categorization: Merchants are auto-mapped to categories (e.g., Swiggy → Food, Uber → Transport, Netflix → Entertainment, Amazon → Shopping).
  • Privacy: Raw SMS text is never stored. Only the extracted amount, category, and date are saved.
phone_android Auto SMS Sync (Android App)
  • Setup: Go to Profile → Enable Auto-Read SMS for Expenses → Copy your SMS Sync Token.
  • Android App: Open MyWealthPilot on your Android device → go to SMS Sync Setup → paste your token and enable.
  • Permissions: The app requests RECEIVE_SMS (for real-time detection) and READ_SMS (for inbox scanning).
  • Real-time: When a new bank SMS arrives, it's automatically detected, parsed, and added as an expense or income.
  • Inbox Scan: Tap "Scan SMS Inbox" to process recent bank SMS (last 7 days or since last scan).
  • Auto-launch Scan: Each time you open the app, it automatically scans for any new bank SMS since the last sync.
  • Deduplication: The server prevents duplicate entries — if the same amount on the same date is already recorded via [SMS Auto], it won't be added again.
  • Bank Filtering: Only SMS from bank/UPI sender IDs containing financial keywords (debited, credited, Rs., INR, etc.) are processed. Personal messages are never read.
security Privacy & Security: SMS data is transmitted over HTTPS. Raw SMS is never stored on our servers. Only extracted transaction details (amount, date, category) are saved. You can disable SMS sync at any time from your Profile.
