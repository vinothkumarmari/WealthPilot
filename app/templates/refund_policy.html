Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Refund Eligibility

We offer a full refund within 7 days of your subscription purchase if you are not satisfied with our service. No questions asked.

  • Refund requests must be made within 7 calendar days from the date of payment.
  • Refunds are processed to the original payment method used during purchase.
  • Refund processing takes 5-7 business days depending on your bank or payment provider.
2. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, send an email to support@mywealthpilot.in with:

  • Your registered email address
  • Razorpay payment/transaction ID (available in your billing history)
  • Reason for refund (optional, helps us improve)
3. Subscription Cancellation
  • You can cancel your subscription at any time. Your premium features will remain active until the end of your current billing period.
  • After the billing period ends, your account will automatically revert to the Free plan.
  • No data is deleted upon downgrade — all your financial records remain intact.
  • You can re-subscribe at any time to regain premium features.
4. Non-Refundable Cases
  • Requests made after 7 days of purchase.
  • Subscriptions purchased with promotional/coupon codes (unless otherwise stated).
  • Account suspension due to violation of Terms of Service.
5. Contact

For refund or cancellation requests, contact us at support@mywealthpilot.in. We aim to respond within 24-48 business hours.
