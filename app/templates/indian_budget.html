TDS & TCS Rationalization
TDS Threshold Increases
  • Interest on securities (193): ₹5,000 → ₹10,000
  • Dividends (194): ₹5,000 → ₹10,000
  • Insurance commission (194D): ₹15,000 → ₹20,000
  • Rent (194-I): ₹2,40,000 → ₹6,00,000
  • Senior citizen FD interest (194A): ₹50,000 → ₹1,00,000
  • Other FD interest (194A): ₹40,000 → ₹50,000
TCS on Foreign Remittance
  • LRS limit for TCS: ₹7,00,000 → ₹10,00,000 (no TCS below this)
  • Education abroad TCS: Removed if financed by loan from specified institution

Capital Gains & Investment Impact
Updated Capital Gains (from Budget 2024)
  • STCG (Equity): 20% (was 15%)
  • LTCG (Equity): 12.5% above ₹1.25L (was 10% above ₹1L)
  • LTCG (Debt/Gold/Property): 12.5% (was 20% with indexation)
  • Indexation: Removed for properties acquired after 23 Jul 2024
NPS Vatsalya (New)
  • NPS accounts can now be opened for minors (children)
  • Converted to regular NPS account when child turns 18
  • Same tax benefits under 80CCD
  • Build retirement corpus from childhood
