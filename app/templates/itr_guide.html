fact_check ITR Filing Checklist (Tax + Refund Ready)
Before Filing
  • Collect Form 16, AIS/TIS, Form 26AS, bank interest certificates.
  • Reconcile salary, TDS, capital gains, business receipts, and other income.
  • Match all deduction proofs (insurance, PPF, NPS, tuition, donations if eligible).
  • Choose correct ITR form (ITR-1/2/3/4 as applicable).
After Filing
  • E-verify immediately (Aadhaar OTP / net banking / DSC).
  • Track refund status and keep filing acknowledgment.
  • Respond quickly to any notice for mismatch.
  • Keep digital folder of all proofs for at least 6 years.
business_center Legal Tax Optimization Ideas for Business Owners / Entrepreneurs
  • Maintain strict bookkeeping and separate personal vs business spend.
  • Claim only genuine business expenses with proper invoices/GST trail.
  • Use presumptive schemes (where eligible) for compliance simplicity.
  • Optimize salary vs profit withdrawal structure with CA guidance.
  • Plan depreciation on assets and eligible capital expenditure correctly.
  • Track advance tax quarterly to avoid interest/penalty.
  • Use eligible retirement/investment products within legal limits.
  • Ensure TDS/TCS compliance for vendors and employees.
  • Use compliant entity structure decisions (proprietorship/LLP/company).
  • Never use fake invoices, shell routing, or undisclosed cash suppression.
For final filing, always validate with your CA or tax professional. This guide is educational and designed for legal tax planning only.
